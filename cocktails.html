The perfect cocktail...

Here at Jive headquarters, we believe in going to the next level with our dancing, our dress, and especially with our drinks. Our crack team of cocktailians are bringing back libations that were forced underground during the "noble experiment" of Prohibition, and cocktail legend Brother Cleve designs new ones that take full advantage of the spirits of the modern-day cocktail renaissance.

Can't wait until our next event? We're excited to share our recipes so that you can make these drinks at home. Need help narrowing down the options? Try our new cocktail search!
